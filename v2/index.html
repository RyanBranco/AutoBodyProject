About us

With the combonation of our 20 years of collision repair experience, and highly trained staff, we are able to restore your vehicle to its pre-accident condition.


We utilize the latest repair equipment available in the auto body industry to ensure the best quality and turn around time possible to our customers. Due to the complexity of today’s vehicles we have an ongoing training program in place to insure the highest standards of quality. We believe that if the entire job is done at our facility we can have a much better control of the quality and the turn around time of the repair.


Our professional and courteous staff, will handle with care all the aspects of the vehicle repair and will always try to go beyond the customers expectations. We have an unparalleled commitment to quality, precision and detail along with pride and honesty, and provide a lifetime warranty on all body and paint work.
